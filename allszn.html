ALL SEASON NAILS

LOGO DESIGN

Overview

All Season is a nail salon located inside Park Royal mall in Vancouver. My task was to refresh their logo and apply it to create a new brochure and business card.

Duration: 2 months

Tools: Adobe Photoshop, Adobe Illustrator, Figma

Initial ideas

I began the process with a rapid ideation session where I generated 3 sets of 15 different patterns for the logo. The rough goal kept in mind during this stage was to consolidate the name "All Season" into a simple, recognizable icon.

Final Design

This was an opportunity where I was able to work with a business in an industry I previously had not designed for before. Consequently, every step of the process was out of my comfort zone, however, in turn, it drove home for me the importance of looking at my own designs from someone else's perspective in order to create a project that speaks to many others.

The final deliverables - a brochure, and business cards.
